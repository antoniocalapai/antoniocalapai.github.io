Cognitive Enrichment for Monkeys

The TaskBar is a touchscreen-based multiple-choice interface enabling monkeys to voluntarily select cognitive enrichment tasks inside their enclosure. It enhances agency, engagement, and supports autonomous high-throughput testing.

The interface presents three large, ergonomically optimised buttons:

  • Static reach – touch a stationary red ball
  • Dynamic reach – touch a moving red ball
  • Social stimuli – view images of conspecifics

Animals developed stable preferences, and periodic repositioning did not affect their choices — indicating genuine voluntary selection.

The system was validated and published in 2023 (Animals), demonstrating reliable psychophysical signatures across difficulty manipulations.
